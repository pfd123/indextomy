About Me

Hopeful, honest, accepting.

I'm a gay man and I bring that lived experience into the room. You don't need to explain yourself here. I work online with adults across the UK, Ireland and Europe, specialising in supporting LGBTQ+ clients navigating identity, anxiety, relationships and shame.

I work with a relational, body-aware approach, drawing on Somatic Experiencing and ecotherapy to help you move at a pace that feels right.

If you've spent years managing, masking, or feeling like the real you takes up too much space, therapy can be a place to unlearn that.
