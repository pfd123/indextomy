Counselling Agreement *
Please read through the agreement below before accepting.

Counselling with me

My name is Peter Donnelly, and I am a qualified counsellor, registered with the British Association of Counselling and Psychotherapy (BACP). I work to the BACP's ethical framework for Counsellors. I hold an enhanced DBS certificate, professional indemnity insurance, and engage in continual training to offer clients the best support possible.

The way I work

There are different types of counselling to choose from and it is important you find the right fit for you. I have trained in the person-centred approach which values your process; trusting you as the expert on your own life. I will support you in finding your own answers.

Counselling Session

Sessions take place through Zoom or by phone and last 50 minutes. You can decide whether to book a phone or Zoom session. There are times where counselling online may not be suitable for your current situation. If I believe that working online, or over the phone is not the most suitable means of support for you I will let you know, and assist in finding a more suitable referral local to you.

Fees and Payment

The fee is £90 per 50 minute session. Fees are payable in advance of the session in order to secure your booking. I have a limited number of reduced rate slots available for those who require it.

Confidentiality

Counselling sessions are strictly confidential between counsellor and client. The exceptions to this are:

1) You have revealed a serious risk to yourself or others
2) You have disclosed information relating to risk to a child or a vulnerable adult
3) You have disclosed information relating to a possible act of terrorism
4) You have disclosed information relating to money laundering or drug trafficking
5) I am required by a Court to disclose information

Where appropriate, I would endeavour to discuss with you what was happening and why.

Record Keeping

I will keep brief notes of the sessions that we have together. I am registered with the Information Commissioners Office (ICO) as a controller of sensitive data and work to their standards for storing any personal information. You have the right to request that I erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

Contacting between sessions

In instances where you need to contact me between sessions you can email me at peter@peterdonnelly.co.uk. I will respond as time permits between sessions. I only use email to communicate information about appointments and bookings; to protect your confidentiality I will not discuss session material. I do not engage socially with clients outside of therapeutic sessions. This includes social media.

This is not a crisis or emergency service. If you need to speak to someone immediately or you or someone you know is in a life-threatening situation, call the emergency services on 999 or 112, NHS on 111, or the Samaritans 24/7 on 116 123.

Non-attendance and cancellations

Sessions cancelled within 24 hours of appointment time will be charged at full rate.

You must accept the agreement to continue.
